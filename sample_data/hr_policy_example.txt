ACME HR Policy (Excerpt)

- Working hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Remote work: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
- Leave: Annual leave entitlement is 18 business days. Submit requests via the HR portal.
- Travel: All travel must be booked through the corporate travel desk.
- IT Help: Email helpdesk@acme.example for urgent support. Average response time: 4 business hours.
